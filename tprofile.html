Dr. Arun Jakhetiya

Position: Assistant Professor of Computer Science

Department: Department of Computer Science

Years of Experience: 10+ years

Biography

Dr. Arun Jakhetiya has been a faculty member at the Indian Institute of Technology, Jammu for over 10 years. He specializes in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning and has contributed to various research projects in the field. His teaching philosophy is focused on making complex concepts accessible to students through real-world examples and interactive learning. Dr. Jakhetiya holds a PhD in Computer Science from the prestigious Cambridge University and has published numerous papers in academic journals.
